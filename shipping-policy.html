Shipping Policy

Last updated: 04 June 2026

Digital-Only Service

Animals With Humanity (AWH Bharat) provides online ticket booking services for events and activities through its website. Since we offer digital tickets and event registrations, no physical products are shipped to customers.

Ticket Delivery

⚡
Standard Delivery
Instant — displayed immediately after payment
Your ticket with QR code appears on-screen as soon as the payment is confirmed.
📱
SMS / Email Confirmation
Sent to your registered contact details
A booking confirmation with your ticket link may also be sent via SMS and/or email after successful payment.
⏱️
Maximum Delivery Time
Up to 24 hours in rare cases
In the unlikely event of a technical issue, ticket delivery may take up to 24 hours. If you have not received your ticket within this window, please contact us.

Retrieving Your Ticket

  • You can access your tickets anytime at ticket.awhbharat.org/my-tickets.html using your registered mobile number.
  • Your ticket contains a unique QR code required for entry — do not share it publicly.
  • Screenshots of the digital ticket are accepted at the venue.
