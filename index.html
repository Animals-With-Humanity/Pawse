Meet Puneeta

Puneeta, an ISB graduate and engineer, has been embracing the art of yoga for a decade. Driven by a passion to cultivate a healthier, calmer, and kinder world, she has dedicated the past 5 years to teaching meditative yoga.

As a loving pet parent to three indie dogs, Puneeta has discovered true joy and peace in their companionship. Rescuing a stranded puppy in the rains, and eventually keeping her forever, not only expanded her family but also brought profound peace and happiness into her home.

Join Puneeta at our upcoming Yoga with puppies workshop, where her experience from previous sessions has shown that the presence of playful puppies elevates the yoga practice, helping participants connect deeply with their inner child and soul. This unique blend of serenity and joy promises a deeply meaningful experience for all.
