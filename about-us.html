About Us

We are a dedicated e-commerce marketplace for pet accessories, pet care products, pet merchandise, and related supplies — built for pet parents who want the best for their companions. Our platform brings together a curated range of trusted products in one convenient, secure place.

Who We Are

AWH Bharat (Animals With Humanity) is a registered non-profit organisation committed to the welfare and well-being of animals across India. Our pet marketplace is an extension of that mission — providing pet owners with access to quality products while channelling proceeds towards animal rescue and care initiatives.

Whether you are a first-time pet parent or a seasoned animal lover, our platform is designed to make shopping for your pets simple, reliable, and meaningful.
